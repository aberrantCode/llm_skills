How-To Guide
HowTo schema
Numbered steps with action verbs. Example per step. HowToStep schema. Result at end.

Validate at validator.schema.org or Google's Rich Results Test. Use JSON-LD in <head> — not inline microdata.
E-E-A-T for AI Discovery
  • Experience — first-person case studies, original screenshots, "we tested X and found Y"
  • Expertise — author bios with credentials, industry-specific terminology, technical depth
  • Authoritativeness — backlinks from trusted sources, industry publication mentions
  • Trustworthiness — contact info visible, about page, HTTPS, accurate dates
  • Freshness — visible publish + last-updated dates. AI prefers recent content.
Platform-Specific Signals
ChatGPT
TL;DR sections, named authors with credentials, original research, multi-intent content (covers related questions).
Perplexity
Original statistics with sources, comparison tables, clean URL slugs, YouTube videos (shown in results).
Claude & Gemini
Logical structure, balanced perspectives, rich schema markup, detailed image alt-text, multimedia with transcripts.
Anti-patterns to avoid: No dates on content — anonymous content (no author = no E-E-A-T) — walls of text (use headers, bullets, boxes) — generic content without original insights — missing schema — no FAQ section — poor URL structure (/p=12345 not /topic-name) — outdated info (review quarterly minimum).
