How AI Engines Choose Answers
01
Consensus
  • Facts across multiple credible sources
  • Repeat key facts consistently
  • Use same terminology as industry leaders
  • Internal content clusters reinforce each other
02
Information Gain
  • Original research and data
  • Concrete examples with specifics
  • Clear point of view (no fence-sitting)
  • Expert quotes with credentials
  • Case studies with metrics
03
Entities & Structure
  • Semantic triples (Subject → Verb → Object)
  • Clear headings with entity names
  • Schema markup (Article, FAQ, Product)
  • Short paragraphs (2–4 sentences)
semantic triple pattern
Semantic Triples
Compact facts AI engines can't misread. Pattern: [Subject] [verb] [object].
✓ Clear triples
HubSpot CRM syncs contact data.
Lead Scoring assigns priority from engagement.
Workflows trigger email sequences from events.
✗ Vague (no entity)
The system helps with various tasks.
It can do many things for users.
This improves overall performance.
Paragraph Pattern
Every substantive paragraph follows Feature → How → Outcome:
[Feature] helps [User/Role] with [Job].
It [mechanism/inputs] to [process].
Teams see [metric/result] in [timeframe].

Triples:
· [Subject] [verb] [object].
· [Subject] [verb] [object].
Per paragraph: 2–4 sentences, 2 triples, specific entities, active voice verbs only.
